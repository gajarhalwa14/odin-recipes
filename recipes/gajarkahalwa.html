Description

Gajar Ka Halwa is a rich, sweet carrot pudding and a traditional Indian dessert.

  • Carrots
  • Milk
  • Almonds
  • Pistachios
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Raisins
  1. Wash and peel Carrots
  2. Add ghee to heat the pan. Then add the nuts and raisins. Fry the nuts until they are lightly golden and the raisins are slightly plump.
  3. Add carrots and sautee for 3 min
  4. Add milk, cover and cook
  5. Add cardamom powder
  6. Add more ghee and keep cooking until milk has evaporated
  7. Serve with additional nuts as garnish!
